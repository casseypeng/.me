Hi! I am Cassey,
An aspirating multi-media designer.

Raised in Foshan, China, I was a traditional Asian girl concentrating in school and homework. When I was in grade 10, I volunteered as a TV producer in my high school TV station which opened my eyes to new media arts. Then my life was involved in various video production, such as TV programs, music videos, and short films. I later on decided to pursue my undergraduate degree overseas and study a wider range of multi-media design.

In my year-long experience in SIAT, I got diverse interest and skills in graphic design, photography, web design, and animation. My life goal is filled with new adventures and challenges every day, and design is one way to pursue it.
